About me

I am Hengli Wang, a second-year Ph.D. candidate of RAM-LAB at the Robotics Institute of Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), supervised by Prof. Ming Liu . I received my B.E. from Zhejiang University (ZJU) in 2018.

Research

My research interests mainly include computer vision and visual navigation on mobile robots. Specifically, I am working on the computation of visual features such as stereo matching, optical flow estimation and surface normal estimation. I am also focusing on improving perception performance and visual navigation performance by exploiting the aforementioned visual features. Representative works are highlighted.
